Reset

Meditation Guide

Use this short routine when cravings spike and you need a slower, steadier rhythm.

Step 01

Ground your body

Sit down, relax your shoulders, and place both feet on the floor. Feel the weight of yourself being held.

Step 02

Breathe with one count

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, and exhale for 6 seconds. Repeat the pattern for a few rounds without rushing.

Step 03

Watch the urge instead of joining it

Silently say: “This urge is temporary. I don’t have to act on it.” Notice it as a wave, not an instruction.

Step 04

Leave with one small action

When you finish, drink water and do one healthy action immediately so your body has a clear next direction.
