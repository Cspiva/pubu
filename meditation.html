Meditation Guide

Use this short routine when cravings spike.

1) Ground your body

Sit down, relax your shoulders, and place both feet on the floor.

2) Breathe rhythm

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 6 seconds. Repeat for 3 minutes.

3) Observe the urge

Silently say: "This urge is temporary. I don't have to act on it."

4) Redirect

After the timer, drink water and do one small healthy action immediately.
