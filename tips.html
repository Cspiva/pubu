Urge Tips

Quick strategies to help you curve the urge.

Delay 10 minutes

Promise yourself you'll wait 10 minutes before deciding anything.

Switch environment

Move to a different room or go outside for a short walk.

Replace the action

Drink water, chew gum, stretch, or wash your face with cool water.

Name the urge

Say: "This is an urge, not a command. It will pass."

Reach out

Text one supportive person: "I'm working through an urge right now."
