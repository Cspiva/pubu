Urge Tips

Quick strategies to help you curb the urge before it grows into action.

Step 01

Delay the impulse

Promise yourself you will wait 10 minutes before deciding anything. Most urges soften when you stop feeding them instantly.

Try: set a short timer and tell yourself, “I can revisit this in ten.”

Step 02

Shift your setting

Move to a different room, stand outside, or even change chairs. A tiny environmental break can interrupt the loop.

  • Open a window
  • Walk to the kitchen
  • Step outside for one minute

Step 03

Give your body another job

Swap the urge with a physical action that is simple and grounding: water, stretching, gum, or cool water on your face.

Drink water Stretch Wash face

Step 04

Name what is happening

Say to yourself: “This is an urge, not a command.” Naming it creates just enough distance to choose differently.

This feeling is temporary. I do not have to obey it.

Step 05

Leave the loop outward

Text one supportive person something short and honest: “I’m working through an urge right now.” Connection changes the energy fast.

You do not need a perfect message. You just need contact.
